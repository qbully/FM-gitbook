# Choosing Your Categories

You don't need every alert — just the ones that matter to you. Category roles control which product alerts ping you, so your feed stays clean and every notification is one you actually care about.

## Picking Your Categories

Head to **select-roles** and tap the categories you want alerts for.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/73409-select-roles.png" alt="Select roles category buttons"><figcaption>Tap a category to turn its alerts on</figcaption></figure>

* Tap a category to turn its alerts **on**
* Tap it again to turn them **off**
* Pick as many as you want, change them anytime

## Which Should You Pick?

Start with the categories you already make content in. Those are the products you can move on fastest. You can always add more once you see what's dropping — and if you want to catch everything, pick them all.
