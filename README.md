# What Is First Movers

First Movers is a TikTok Shop product-alert service built for affiliates. We monitor hundreds of the top TikTok Shop brands around the clock and alert you the moment a product drops or starts moving — so you know first, post first, and grab the ad spend before other affiliates even know the product exists.

The whole service is built around one idea: **speed wins.** On TikTok Shop today, being early to a product is the difference between riding a wave and chasing one. First Movers exists to make sure you're early — every time.

* **Real-time alerts** across every major product category
* **Coverage of the top GMV Max brands** on TikTok Shop
* **24/7 automated monitoring** — you never have to watch for drops yourself
* **Retail matching** — when a product is also stocked at a major retailer, we hand you a direct link so you can grab it locally and film today
* **Category channels** so you only get pinged for the products you actually care about
