## The GMV Max Era

TikTok Shop moved to a GMV Max model, an automated ad system that pushes budget behind products that are converting. Brands set their targets, TikTok finds the content driving sales, and it pours ad spend into whatever is working.

For affiliates, this means the game is speed. Every time a top brand drops a new product, variant, flavor, or bundle, there is a short window where GMV Max budgets are hunting for content. Catch that window and your commissions stack. Miss it and you are posting into a saturated feed where everyone is already selling the same thing.

While everyone else is manually checking Kalodata and Fastmoss for new winners that take days to update, First Movers already sent the alert.

## Notification Guide

First Movers only works if you actually see the alerts when they fire. It is very important that your notifications are set up properly so you never miss a window.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73835-notifications.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For every category channel you subscribed to, right-click the channel (or long-press on mobile), select **Notification Settings**, and set it to **All Messages**. Do this for each category you picked, plus [`🔥┃trending-products`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1490144082830430298).

**Be sure to enable push notifications in the Discord mobile app.** Speed is the whole game, and being able to act on an alert as fast as possible is what puts you ahead of other affiliates. Make sure Discord notifications are enabled in your phone system settings, and that the First Movers server is not muted.

## Reading an Alert

Every alert is built to get you from a product dropping to filming it as fast as possible.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73922-alert.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Each alert contains:

* **Brand:** the brand behind the product.
* **Product:** the full product name.
* **Commission:** the payout rate and rough dollar amount per sale, when available. Always double-check the live rate on the listing before you commit.
* **View Product:** opens the live TikTok Shop listing so you can grab it and start creating.
* **Image:** images of the product.

## Fresh Drops vs Trending

First Movers gives you two kinds of alerts, for two different plays.

The category channels post **Fresh Drops**, which alert you the moment a brand releases a brand new product, before it has trended, before it is obvious, before anyone has filmed it. These come from top GMV Max brands with real ad budgets and loyal buyers, which means they are built to sell. You are getting in at zero. The same product every affiliate will be fighting over weeks from now was posted here first.

The [`🔥┃trending-products`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1490144082830430298) channel posts products that are already racking up sales right out the gate. Just released, still early, already selling, and most affiliates have not noticed yet.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73949-trending-alert.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Trending alerts include the sales data, so you can see exactly how fast a product is moving before you commit. The trending channel is a forum, so you can filter by category to find what is moving in your lane.

## Beat the Shipping

This is an edge no other service gives you. When we find signals that the same product may be sitting on a shelf at a major retailer, we drop a link right on the alert that takes you straight to it.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/74145-retail-buttons.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

A button like **Possible stock near you** or **Possible stock at a store** means we found the same product at that retailer, such as Walmart, Target, Ulta, and more. Instead of waiting days for TikTok Shop shipping, you can grab it locally or fast-ship it and film today, while affiliates still waiting on their package fall behind.

The play is simple. An alert fires with a button. You tap it and check the listing. You grab it locally or fast-ship it. You film today and post before the product saturates. Availability can vary by location, so it is worth a quick stock check before you make a trip.

If you already run the in-store method, where you film the product right there in the store, the buttons get you to it faster so you can post the same day without waiting on shipping.
