# Getting Access

Getting into First Movers takes a couple of minutes.

First, head to the [`💎┃get-access`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1440403968604901558) channel and follow the link. It takes you to our Whop page where you connect your account and unlock the server.

During setup you will connect your Discord account. This unlocks the alert channels, and once connected the full server opens up.

Once you are in, head to [`🖱️┃select-roles`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1448832506706133166) and choose which product categories you want alerts for. Then set up your notifications so alerts reach you in real time. An alert you see late is an alert you missed.
