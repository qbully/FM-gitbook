# 🔔 Setting Up Notifications

First Movers only works if you actually see the alerts when they fire. A product you find out about an hour late is a product someone else already posted. Set your notifications so alerts reach you in real time.

## Turn On Notifications for Alert Channels

For every category channel you picked, set it to notify you on **every message** — not just mentions.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73835-notifications.png" alt="Channel notification settings"><figcaption>Right-click a channel to open its notification settings</figcaption></figure>

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) the channel → **Notification Settings** → **All Messages**.

Do this for every category you picked in [🖱️│select-roles](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1448832506706133166), plus [🔥│trending-products](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1490144082830430298).

## 📱 Mobile Is Your Edge

Turn on push notifications in the Discord mobile app. Speed is the whole game — acting on an alert the moment it fires, wherever you are, is what puts you ahead of affiliates waiting at a desk.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Make sure Discord notifications are enabled in your **phone's system settings** too, and that the First Movers server isn't muted. A muted server means missed windows.
{% endhint %}
