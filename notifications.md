# Setting Up Notifications

First Movers only works if you actually see the alerts when they fire. A product you find out about an hour late is a product someone else already posted. Set your notifications so the alerts reach you in real time.

## Turn On Notifications for Alert Channels

For any category channel you care about, set it to notify you on every message — not just mentions.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/73835-notifications.png" alt="Channel notification settings"><figcaption>Right-click a channel to open its notification settings</figcaption></figure>

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) the channel → **Notification Settings** → set it to **All Messages**.

Do this for every category channel you picked in select-roles, plus **trending-products**.

## Mobile Is Your Edge

Turn on push notifications in the Discord mobile app. Speed is the whole game — being able to act on an alert the moment it fires, wherever you are, is what puts you ahead of affiliates sitting at a desk waiting to check.

Make sure Discord notifications are enabled in your phone's system settings, and that the First Movers server isn't muted.

## Don't Miss Announcements

Keep notifications on for announcements too, so you catch any updates to the service.
