# The GMV Max Era

To understand why First Movers works, you have to understand how TikTok Shop changed.

## What Changed

TikTok Shop moved to a **GMV Max** model — an automated ad system that pushes budget behind products that are converting. Brands set their targets, TikTok's system finds the content driving sales, and it pours ad spend into whatever's working.

For affiliates, this changed the game entirely. It's no longer about guessing what might go viral. It's about being **early on the right products** — the ones from brands with real budgets and real demand — because when those products start moving, the system rewards the creators who were already there.

## Why Being First Matters Now

Under GMV Max, the early content on a product wins. When a product starts converting, TikTok's system amplifies the creators posting about it. If you're one of the first to post, you catch that wave as it builds. If you're late, you're posting into a saturated feed where everyone's already selling the same thing.

The window between "a product drops" and "everyone's on it" is short — sometimes days. That window is the entire opportunity. First Movers exists to put you inside it, every time, instead of finding out after it's closed.

## The Bottom Line

Whoever posts a product **first** catches the views, the sales, and the ad spend. The GMV Max era rewards speed. First Movers makes sure that speed is yours.
