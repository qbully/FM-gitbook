# Beat the Shipping

This is an edge no other service gives you. When we find signals that the same product may be sitting on a shelf at a major retailer, we drop a link right on the alert that takes you straight to it.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/74145-retail-buttons.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

A button like **Possible stock near you** or **Possible stock at a store** means we found the same product at that retailer, such as Walmart, Target, Ulta, and more. Instead of waiting days for TikTok Shop shipping, you can grab it locally or fast-ship it and film today, while affiliates still waiting on their package fall behind.

The play is simple. An alert fires with a button. You tap it and check the listing. You grab it locally or fast-ship it. You film today and post before the product saturates. Availability can vary by location, so it is worth a quick stock check before you make a trip.

If you already run the in-store method, where you film the product right there in the store, the buttons get you to it faster so you can post the same day without waiting on shipping.
