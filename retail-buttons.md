# The Retail Buttons

This is the edge no other alert service gives you.

When a product we alert on is also stocked at a major retailer, we find it and add buttons to the alert linking straight to it.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/74145-retail-buttons.png" alt="Alert with retail buttons"><figcaption>Retail buttons under a product alert</figcaption></figure>

## What They Mean

A button like **Possible stock near you** or **Possible stock at [store]** means we found the same product at that retailer — Walmart, Target, and more.

Instead of waiting days for TikTok Shop shipping, you can grab it locally or fast-ship it and film **today** — while affiliates still waiting on their package fall behind.

## Why This Wins

The whole game is speed. The affiliate who films and posts first catches the wave. If you're waiting on a package, you're already behind the person who walked into a store and grabbed it the same day the alert fired.

The play:

1. Alert fires with a retail button
2. Tap it, check the listing
3. Grab it locally or fast-ship it
4. Film today
5. Post before the product saturates

## A Note on Matches

The retail match is the same product we found at that store. Availability can vary by location, so it's worth a quick check before you make a trip — but when it's there, it's the fastest way to get the product in your hands and get filming.
