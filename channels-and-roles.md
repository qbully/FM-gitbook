# Channels & Roles

## Channels

When you join First Movers, you get access to the full server. Everything is grouped into categories on the left side of the server.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73056-sidebar.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The main categories you will use:

* **WELCOME** contains everything you need to get set up, including [`💎┃get-access`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1440403968604901558), [`📕┃how-to-use`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1520819960207769771), and [`🖱️┃select-roles`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1448832506706133166).
* **PRODUCT ALERTS** is where the alerts land. This is the core of the service.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73157-product-alerts.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Roles

Head to [`🖱️┃select-roles`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1448832506706133166) to choose which product categories you want to be alerted for. You only get pinged for the categories you pick, so your feed stays clean and every alert is one you actually care about.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73409-select-roles.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Attached is a breakdown of the categories you can subscribe to:

* 💊 **Health & Wellness:** Supplements, vitamins, and wellness product drops.
* 💄 **Beauty & Personal Care:** Makeup, skincare, haircare, and personal care drops.
* 🍫 **Food & Beverages:** Snacks, drinks, and food product drops.
* 👗 **Women's Fashion:** Women's clothing and apparel drops.
* 👔 **Men's Clothing:** Men's clothing and apparel drops.
* 🏠 **Lifestyle, Home & Gear:** Home goods, gear, and lifestyle product drops.
* 🏃‍♀️ **Sports & Outdoors:** Fitness, sports, and outdoor product drops.
* 🐱 **Pets:** Pet food, toys, and accessory drops.
* 👟 **Footwear:** Shoe and footwear drops.
* 🍼 **Maternity & Kids:** New product drops in the baby and kids space.
* 🧩 **Others:** Everything else worth catching.

Tap a category to turn its alerts on. Tap it again to turn them off. You can change your selection at any time.
