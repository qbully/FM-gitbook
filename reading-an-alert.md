# 🚨 Reading an Alert

Every alert is built to get you from "a product dropped" to "I'm filming it" as fast as possible. Here's how to read one.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73922-alert.png" alt="Product alert example"><figcaption>A standard product alert</figcaption></figure>

## What's In an Alert

* **Brand** — the brand behind the product
* **Product** — the full product name, including size, variant, and details
* **Commission** — the payout rate and rough dollar amount per sale, when available
* 👉 **View Product** — opens the live TikTok Shop listing so you can grab it and start creating

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Always double-check the live commission rate** on the listing before you commit — rates can change after the alert fires.
{% endhint %}

## The Image

The product image is what you're making content on. It's the first thing to look at — if it's something you can film well, that's half the decision.

## Category Pings

Each alert posts in its category channel and pings the matching role, so you know instantly which lane it's in. If it's a category you didn't pick in [🖱️│select-roles](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1448832506706133166), you won't get pinged — that's the point.

{% hint style="success" %}
An alert is a signal to act **now.** The faster you go from alert to posted content, the more of the window you catch. Check the product — if it fits your lane, move.
{% endhint %}
