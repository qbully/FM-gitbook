# Reading an Alert

Every alert is built to get you from a product dropping to filming it as fast as possible.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73922-alert.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Each alert contains:

* **Brand:** the brand behind the product.
* **Product:** the full product name.
* **Commission:** the payout rate and rough dollar amount per sale, when available. Always double-check the live rate on the listing before you commit.
* **View Product:** opens the live TikTok Shop listing so you can grab it and start creating.
* **Image:** images of the product.

## Fresh Drops vs Trending

First Movers gives you two kinds of alerts, for two different plays.

The category channels post **Fresh Drops**, which alert you the moment a brand releases a brand new product, before it has trended, before it is obvious, before anyone has filmed it. These come from top GMV Max brands with real ad budgets and loyal buyers, which means they are built to sell. You are getting in at zero. The same product every affiliate will be fighting over weeks from now was posted here first.

The [`🔥┃trending-products`](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1490144082830430298) channel posts products that are already racking up sales right out the gate. Just released, still early, already selling, and most affiliates have not noticed yet.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/73949-trending-alert.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Trending alerts include the sales data, so you can see exactly how fast a product is moving before you commit. The trending channel is a forum, so you can filter by category to find what is moving in your lane.
