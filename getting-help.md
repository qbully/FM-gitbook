# Getting Help

Stuck on something? We've got you.

## Open a Ticket

Head to the **open-a-ticket** channel and start a ticket. Tell us what's going on — access issues, questions about an alert, anything — and the team will get you sorted.

## Before You Ticket

A lot of common questions are already answered in this guide:

* Can't get in? See [Getting Access](../getting-started/getting-access.md)
* Not getting alerts? Check your categories in [Choosing Your Categories](../getting-started/choosing-your-categories.md) and your [Notifications](../getting-started/notifications.md)
* Not sure how to read an alert? See [Reading an Alert](../using-alerts/reading-an-alert.md)

If the guide doesn't cover it, open a ticket and we'll help you out.
