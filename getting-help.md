# 🎫 Getting Help

Stuck on something? We've got you.

## Open a Ticket

Head to [🎟️│open-a-ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1440209241192271964/1440222258613321768) and start a ticket. Tell us what's going on — access issues, questions about an alert, anything — and the team will get you sorted.

## Before You Ticket

A lot of common questions are already answered in this guide:

* 💎 Can't get in? See [Getting Access](getting-access.md)
* 🔕 Not getting alerts? Check [Choosing Your Categories](choosing-your-categories.md) and [Notifications](notifications.md)
* 🚨 Not sure how to read an alert? See [Reading an Alert](reading-an-alert.md)

If the guide doesn't cover it, open a ticket and we'll help you out.
